Eggless Mayonnaise Sandwiches

prep time

5 min.

cook time

10 min

serving

3 servings

Ingredients
  • 0.5 cup eggless mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp green capsicum (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp red capsicum (finely chopped)
  • 0.25 cup carrot (grated)
  • 2 tbsp corn (boiled)
  • 0.25 tsp pepper (crushed)
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 6 slices bread (white / brown)
  • 6 tsp green chutney
  • 3 tsp butter
    •

    Instructions
    • Step 1: In a large mixing bowl take 0.5 cup eggless mayonnaise.
    • Step 2: Add in 2 tbsp green capsicum, 2 tbsp red capsicum, 0.25 cup carrot, 2 tbsp corn, 0.25 tsp pepper and 0.25 tsp salt.
    • Step 3: Mix well making sure everything is combined well.
    • Step 4: Spread a tsp of green chutney over a slice of bread.
    • Step 5: Spread 2 tbsp of prepared mayonnaise spread.
    • Step 6: Spread 1 tsp of more green chutney over another bread slice and place over stuffing.
    • Step 7: Toast the sandwich with butter on both sides.
    • Step 8: Finally, cut the bread into half and serve sandwich.
