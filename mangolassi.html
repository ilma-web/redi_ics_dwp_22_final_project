Mango Lassi

prep time

15 min.

cook time

--

serving

3 servings

Ingredients
  • Ripe mango pulp( Alphanso mango) - 1 cup
  • Yogurt/curd(plain)- 1 cup
  • Sugar-50 gm
  • Cardamom powder-1/3 tsp
  • Ice cubes-10 to12
  • Chopped cashew and pistachio- 2 tbsp
    •

    Instructions
    • Step 1: Add the 1 cup mango pulp in the blender.
    • Step 2: Add the 1 cup Yogurt in the blender.
    • Step 3: Put in the ice cubes also.
    • Step 4: Add sugar in the blender.
    • Step 5: Add cardamom powder.
    • Step 6: Put the lid on.
    • Step 7: Blend this untill smooth n thick.
    • Step 8: Take it out in glass.
    • Step 9: Garnuish with chopped cashew and pistachio.
    • Step 10: Serve it cold!.
