Butter Chicken

prep time

15 min.

cook time

1 hr.

serving

3 servings

Ingredients
For Chicken Tikka
  • Chicken boneless - 400 gm
  • Salt-to taste
  • Ginger garlic paste -1 tbsp
  • Lemon juice - 2 tbsp
  • Mustard oil - 4 tbsp
  • Kashmiri chilli powder - 1.5 tbsp
  • Hung curd - 1 cup
    •
    For Curry
  • Water - 2 cups
  • Tomato diced -500 gm
  • Onion sliced - 50 gm
  • Bay leaf - 1 no
  • Cinnamon - 1 stick
  • Black cardamom - 1 no
  • Cloves - 3 no
  • Cardamom - 3 no
  • Garlic cloves - 8 no
  • Ginger - 1 small piece
  • Kashmiri chilli powder - 1 tbsp
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Cashew nuts - 12 no
    •
    for pan
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Ginger chopped - 0.5 tbsp
  • Green chilli - 1 no
  • Fenugreek leaves - 0.5 tsp
  • Sugar - 1/2 tbsp
  • Cream - 2 tbsp
    •

    Instructions
    • Step 1: For chicken tikka pat dry the chicken. Add salt, lemon juice and ginger & garlic paste. Keep aside for at least 20 mins.
    • Step 2: In a bowl add mustard oil & chilly powder and whisk together. Now add hung curd, salt and lemon juice.
    • Step 3: Whisk and then add chicken to this marinade.
    • Step 4: Heat a grill pan on high heat, sprinkle some oil on the hot pan and grill the chicken till it is done.
    • Step 5: Remove and keep aside.
    • Step 6: Alternatively, you can cook the chicken in preheated oven at 180c for 20 mins and remove it.
    • Step 7: For the curry mix all the ingredients and add them to a clean deep vessel pan. Bring it to a boil. Cover and cook for 20 mins after the boil.
    • Step 8: Now remove and let it cook for few minutes.
    • Step 9: Add it to a blender and make a fine puree.
    • Step 10: Strain and keep aside the puree.
    • Step 11: In a separate pan melt butter and saute chopped ginger and chopped green chilly.
    • Step 12: Add the curry and some water to get the right consistency.
    • Step 13: Add fenugreek leaves powder, sugar and salt to taste.
    • Step 14: Add the cooked chicken into the curry and bring to a boil.
    • Step 15: Finish with fresh cream. Serve hot.
